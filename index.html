About Me

Welcome to My World of Music and Words

Nirmala Mainali is a talented Nepali singer and writer whose work resonates with emotion and cultural richness. With a voice that captures the essence of traditional Nepali music while embracing contemporary sounds, she has established herself as a unique voice in the industry.

Her journey in music began at a young age, influenced by the rich musical heritage of Nepal. Over the years, she has developed a distinctive style that blends traditional melodies with modern arrangements, creating songs that speak to both young and old audiences.

Beyond music, Nirmala is also an accomplished writer, expressing her thoughts and experiences through poetry and prose that reflect the Nepali cultural landscape.
